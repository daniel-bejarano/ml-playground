News Headline Classifier

The very first model available is a News Headline Classifier. It asks you to provide a news headline and returns a label: "left", "right" or "center". Each label represents the model's prediction of the political inclination of the source for that particular headline.

Although the accuracy of the model is only at about 42%, it still is a little improvement from random guessing (which would give us 33.3% accuracy).
